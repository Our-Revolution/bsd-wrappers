How To Get Started

  1. Watch the orientation video or read the step-by-step guide to learn how to phone bank for Our Revolution.
  2. View the script by clicking 'Calling Script' in the sidebar.
  3. Get your login credentials. You'll need to do this each day before you make calls, as logins are renewed daily.
  4. Log in during our calling hours (view in sidebar on right).
  5. After logging in, switch your status to "ready” and start talking to voters!
