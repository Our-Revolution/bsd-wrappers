Data Architect and Coordinator

Our Revolution, the successor organization to the Bernie 2016 presidential campaign, is seeking a talented individual to serve as the organization’s Data Coordinator. Ideal candidates will be wholly committed to the organization's mission of creating a more socially, environmentally, economically, and racially equitable nation by winning key progressive victories. Additionally, they will exhibit a "not me, us" attitude, working as a team to achieve the common goal. Our Revolution is an equal opportunity employer that strongly values and looks to continue developing the diversity of our team. Women, persons of color, LGBTQ individuals, veterans, and members of other underrepresented groups are strongly encouraged to apply.

Summary:

The Data Coordinator will serve a key role in ensuring that staff and volunteers working to support Our Revolution receive the data support needed to engage in our progressive fights. Candidate will be responsible for organization-wide data analysis and support. Background in analytics preferred. Candidate will report directly to the Executive Director.

Responsibilities

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Work efficiently with outside vendors to manage all data (Vertica, Blue State Digital, Voter Activation Network databases, among other)
  • Provide support and strategic data analysis to the needed departmental teams
  • Perform regular results-based data compilation and analysis
  • Supply and support reporting and data needs of staff, making timely, fact-based recommendation
  • Manage transfer of campaign's voter file to new entity
  • Work with digital team to evaluate the success of email, social media, website, and online ad content and strategies to maximize fundraising and participation in our programs.
  • Other tasks as assigned

Qualifications:

  • Demonstrated expertise in VAN administration
  • Advanced knowledge of Excel or similar application(s)
  • Strong communication and presentation skills, including technical communication
  • Strong time management skills
  • Extensive SQL knowledge and experience
  • API development experience
  • Familiarity with digital organizing techniques
  • Familiarity with GIS

Salary:

$60,000 - $65,000 annually

Resume and cover letter should be submitted to ourrev.us/jobs with Data Coordinator selected in the dropdown box. Applications will be considered on a rolling basis.
